About Us

The primary objective of Currency Balancing System (CBS) is to create a secure and user-friendly crypto e-commerce system that allows customers to purchase daily essentials using cryptocurrency.

As the stabilization by Stable Coin is limited to their own Cryptocurrency price instead of balancing the price of a single and cash-centralized entity, CBS is backed by real-life products and numerous trustworthy enterprises. The CBS approach is suitable for establishing an unfluctuating crypto value of a daily life product and can be deployed in the form of decentralization amongst individual corporations.

How CBS Work?

We had build a CBS algorithm to identify if the product price is reasonable or not.

Theory

CBS will keep tracking the value of Cryptocurrency and filter the noise. The price of the product will be keep within +25% to -25% from it orginally price. New price will only be keep when the price are within this range.

CBS Profit

5% handling fee are cast per each transation. The profit will be used on the stabilization of product price and CBS. We aims to make Cryptocurrency becomes a widely used payment method and achieve the popularization of the cryptocurrency.
